The Lattice Approach

A lattice is a structure built for stability through interconnected, independent nodes. At LTS, our investment framework mirrors this principle. We develop and run a portfolio of uncorrelated trading algorithms. By combining momentum and mean reversion factors across multiple asset classes, we seek to optimize risk-adjusted returns.

Our strategy relies entirely on probabilistic models and automated execution. We adjust exposures dynamically based on the current market regime, removing the guesswork, ego, and "macro forecasting" that often lead to inconsistent results.

Core Principles

  • Systematic Execution: Eliminating behavioral bias through fully automated, rules-based trade execution.
  • Diversified Strategies: Deploying uncorrelated independent models to ensure systemic resilience across varying market conditions.
  • Strict Risk Management: Prioritizing dynamic capital allocation and strict drawdown limits to protect capital in adverse regimes.
