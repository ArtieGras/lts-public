Our Philosophy: The Lattice Approach

A lattice is a foundational structure defined by its interconnected elements. At LTS, our investment framework mirrors this principle. We deploy a diversified, rules-based approach focused on building a portfolio of completely uncorrelated trading algorithms. By converging momentum, mean reversion, and statistical arbitrage models into a cohesive system, we prioritize disciplined volatility control and optimized risk-adjusted metrics, such as the Sharpe ratio. We do not rely on discretionary macro calls; we rely on mathematics.

Our Infrastructure & Execution

Enduring success in modern markets requires a holistic, systemic view converging with an institutional-grade operational edge. Headquartered in St. Petersburg, Florida, LTS leverages advanced, automated technology—including algorithmic execution via Composer and secure custody through Alpaca—to deploy high-fidelity systematic strategies. This infrastructure allows our models to dynamically adapt to shifting market regimes while maintaining strict downside protection.
