Our Philosophy: Why "Lattice"?

A lattice is a foundational structure defined by its interconnected elements. It represents order, geometric stability, and distributed strength. At LTS, our approach to trading mirrors this principle. We don't rely on fragile, isolated algorithms; instead, we build comprehensive trading lattices—robust frameworks where diverse, quantitative strategies intersect. This structure provides systemic resilience, optimizing performance while fundamentally mitigating risk.

Our Origin

Lattice Trading Systems was founded in St. Petersburg, Florida, by partners with deep backgrounds in complex systems engineering and quantitative finance. Our inception was driven by a single observation: that enduring success in modern, high-frequency markets requires a holistic, systemic view rather than a collection of independent trades. We set out to build a firm where mathematical precision is unified within a cohesive, stable framework—converging systemic stability with operational edge.

Our Business

Lattice Trading Systems, LLC is a proprietary trading firm. We specialize exclusively in the development and execution of quantitative, algorithmic trading strategies across multiple global asset classes. Our operations focus on capturing market inefficiencies through high-speed data analysis and systematic execution.
