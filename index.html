Proprietary Algorithmic Trading: The Lattice Approach

A lattice is a structure built for stability through interconnected, independent nodes. At Lattice Trading Systems (LTS), our investment framework mirrors this architecture by treating each algorithmic strategy as an independent model of market behavior. Rather than relying on a single assumption, we synthesize diverse "nodes" rooted in various market factors into an integrated structure.

The strength of the lattice lies in its orthogonal connections — defined by intentional uncorrelation. By ensuring our strategies move at "right angles" to one another, we create structural integrity: when one model faces stress in a specific regime, the others provide support to maintain the stability of the entire system. We move beyond isolated trade-picking to engineer a resilient framework designed to survive and thrive across any market environment.

Core Principles

  • Diversified Alpha: Deploying uncorrelated independent models to ensure systemic resilience and stability across varying market conditions.
  • Systematic Execution: Eliminating behavioral bias through fully automated, rules-based trade execution.
  • Strict Risk Management: Prioritizing dynamic capital allocation and rigorous drawdown limits to protect capital in adverse regimes.
